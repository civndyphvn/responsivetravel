Places to See!

There are many things to see in Paris! Check out beautiful architects Paris have to offer. A few of there most famous architectural buildings are:The Louvre,Arc de Triomphe, Chateau de Versailles, Palais Garnier, etc.

The Louvre

The Louvre, or the Louvre Museum, is the world's most-visited museum, and a historic landmark in Paris, France. It is the home of some of the best-known works of art, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Palais Garnier

The Palais Garnier, also known as Opéra Garnier, is a 1,979-seat opera house at the Place de l'Opéra in the 9th arrondissement of Paris, France.

Chateau de Versailles

The Palace of Versailles is a former royal residence located in Versailles, about 12 miles west of Paris, France.

Places to Dine!

Enjoy some delicious food in Paris with great energy and view. Go on an adventure and try some local restaurants and pastries that is to die for!

Cafe de Flore

If you love historic charm, visit Café de Flore! Café de Flore is one of the oldest cafes in Paris and it is frequented by locals and celebrities! Café de Flore is surrounded by flowers on its awning, which makes the place feels vibrant and alive.

Maison Rose

Famous for it's pink building. Maison Rose serve traditional French Cuisine that is to die for!
